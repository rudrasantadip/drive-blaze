Hack For Blaze 1.0

Hack for Blaze 1.0 is an exciting hackathon that challenges participants to innovate and create solutions across diverse and impactful themes. Teams can focus on Mental Health/Fitness, developing tools that promote well-being and healthy lifestyles, or delve into Renewable/Green Energy, driving sustainability through innovative energy solutions.The Gamification of Learning theme invites participants to make education engaging and fun, while Fintech Education seeks to demystify finance for all. Smart Education challenges teams to enhance learning through technology, and Disaster Management calls for solutions that improve response and recovery efforts during crises. Whether hardware, software, or a combination of both, Hack for Blaze 1.0 is a platform for creative minds to make a real-world impact.

Themes

  • Mental Health / Fitness
  • Renewable / Green Energy
  • Gamification of learning
  • Fintech Education
  • Smart Education
  • Disaster management.
